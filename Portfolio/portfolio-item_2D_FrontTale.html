Unity2D FrontTale

2D TowerDefense - Mobile

This game was created from a small tutorial that I then developed further. The player has the opportunity to experience different levels, stores, opponents and much more.

-- Features: --
* different levels
* varied towers
* optimized for mobile
* many and different enemies
* music and special sound effects
* suitable for children and all ages
* Level Menu
* Shop and Upgrades for turrets!
* different archivments!


-- Game description: --
* place turrets on specials points
* defend your base
* click on a tower to upgrade it
* earn money by killing enemies
* don´t die
