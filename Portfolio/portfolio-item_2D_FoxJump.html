Unity2D Fox Jump

2D Platformer

Fox Jump is a jump & run game with varied levels. The player has the possibility to choose between different levels and has to avoid traps and enemies.

-- Features: --
* different and varied levels
* a few small puzzles
* simple controls
* small Easter Eggs
* music and sound effects
* suitable for children and all ages
* different enemies / traps
* achievements
* collecting Gems
* constantly new content (with updates)

-- Planned Features: --
* new and much more varied levels
* new enemies
* new soundtracks / music
* highscore list
* character customization
* balancing
* store for gems

-- Game description: --
* jump by pressing the Spacebar
* double jump by pressing space side twice
* A-D for character movement
* Collect Gems for cracking the respective level highscore
* Cherries can be eaten when the player has lost lives
* Enemies can be eliminated when the player jumps on the respective enemies
* enemies do damage to the player of course


Info: Development is paused!.
