2D Dungen Crawler

this is my little 2D dungeon game The player tries to defeat 3 randomly generated levels and the final boss. Besides he has the possibility to find or buy different weapons and HP upgrades! The player can also choose between different characters that he can unlock during the game or right at the beginning. Each character has its advantages and disadvantages! This game was already longer with me in the development and has taken a lot of time. It will be worked on again and again (that's why it's BETA), but it's on my backlist right now. Bugs, errors or ideas can be submitted to me.
