Zombie Shooter

Alpha - 3D Shooter

It is a small 3D zombie game. You have to try to survive the certain time and then use the helicopter to save them. The zombies are getting stronger and stronger. There is a tutorial, mission (mission is limited number of zombies ~320) and Endless (infinite zombie spawners (~1000)). It is my first game that I developed freely according to my creativity. It was created over the weekend via a custom posed challenge. Time pressure was the biggest factor in this. And yes, it's intentional that the zombies don't make steps (have scared myself so many times because of that :D)

-- Features: --
* different levels
* survive hordes of zombies
* simple controls
* easter eggs
* tutorial
* music and sound effects
